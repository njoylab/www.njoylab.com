## Privacy Policy — njoylab.com

*Last updated: April 2, 2026*

### What data is collected

This site uses **Cloudflare Web Analytics** to measure traffic. Cloudflare collects anonymous, aggregated data such as:

- Page views and navigation paths
- Referring URLs
- Browser type and operating system
- Country of origin (IP addresses are processed transiently by Cloudflare to derive the country, but are not stored or logged by this site)

No personal data is stored. No individual user is tracked.

### Cookies

This site **does not use cookies** of any kind. No tracking cookies, no advertising cookies, no analytics cookies.

### Third-party services

| Service | Purpose | Privacy info |
|---|---|---|
| Cloudflare Web Analytics | Anonymous site traffic statistics | [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/) |
| Cloudflare CDN | Content delivery and DDoS protection | [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/) |

Cloudflare acts as a data processor and handles data in accordance with GDPR. No data is shared with other third parties.

### Your rights (GDPR)

Since no personal data is collected or stored by this site, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. For questions about data processed by Cloudflare, refer to [Cloudflare's privacy policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).
